Blog

Here I will share some places I have visited and some information about them.

Cuba, Havana

  • Location: Cuba is located in the Caribbean Sea, between the Gulf of Mexico and the North Atlantic Ocean. It is 90 miles (150 km) south of Florida. The closest countries to Cuba are Jamaica to the south, and Haiti to the east.
  • Capital City: Havana
  • Temperature Range: 71.6°F (22°C) to 81°F (27.2°C).
  • Official Language: Spanish

France, Paris

  • Location: France is the largest country in Western Europe. Its lengthy borders touch Germany and Belgium in the north; the Atlantic Ocean in the west; the Pyrenees Mountains and Spain in the south.
  • Capital City: Paris
  • Temperature Range: Winter: from 32° F to 46° F, summer: from 61° F to 75° F.
  • Official Language: French

Greece, Santorini

  • Location: Greece is located in Southern Europe, bordering the Ionian Sea and the Mediterranean Sea, between Albania and Turkey. It is a peninsular country, with an archipelago of about 3,000 islands.
  • Capital City: Athens
  • Climate: The climate of Santorini is Mediterranean with mild, rainy winters and warm, sunny summers.
  • Official Language: Greek

Italy, Venice

  • Location: Italy is located in south-central Europe, occupying a peninsula that juts deep into the Mediterranean Sea. Italy is often described as a country shaped like a boot.
  • Capital City: Rome
  • Climate: With its hot, dry summers and cool, wet winters, Italy experiences a mediterranean climate.
  • Official Language: Italian
